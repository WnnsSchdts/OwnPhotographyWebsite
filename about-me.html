Who I am

Hi, my name is Wannes. I was born and raised in Belgium.
By day, I'm an engineer, working with 3D models of large buildings.
br>Interesting? Maybe.

One thing is certain: it lacks creativity.
Thankfully, I find the creativity I need in photography.

I take pictures—capturing things and themes that interest me. Buildings, landscapes, nightlife—each photo unique, each one carefully taken.

My Journey

My photography journey started in 2011. For my birthday, I received a camera from my stepmother—a Lumix bridge camera.

Nothing special, but perfect for my first steps. I started taking pictures during the holidays.

We traveled to France, Norway, Poland, and Croatia. Later on, I began traveling by myself. Thousands and thousands of pictures, taken by me, each telling a story.
br>But I wasn’t happy with the quality of my pictures. So, I started taking photography courses at KISP.

I learned all the tips and tricks from the wonderful teachers. Now,it’s time to show the world what I’ve developed over these years

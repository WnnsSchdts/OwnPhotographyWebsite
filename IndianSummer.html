Indian Summer

The air was warm, and the sun lingered in the autumn sky, casting a golden glow.

In the countryside, people enjoy the gentle warmth, finding solace in their garden’s embrace.

But in the city, the heat is relentless. Skyscrapers tower, catching the wind, yet soaking in the sun's unforgiving rays.

What’s it like to live in a lofty tower, in a small apartment, without our modern loophole, called airconditioning?

Could this be the very image of hell?

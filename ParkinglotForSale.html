Parkinglot For Sale

Having recently moved to a new part of the city, I discovered a brand-new, modern parking lot.

Due to a gap in the city's mobility plan, the parking lot remains empty most of the time.

A place supposedly in a continuous state of action—like a beehive—now resonates with peace and silence in a city that never sleeps.

Who I Am

Hi, my name is Wannes. I was born and raised in Belgium. By day, I'm an engineer, working with 3D models of large buildings. Interesting? Maybe. It depends on how you look at it. But one thing is certain: it lacks creativity. Thankfully, I find the creativity I need in photography.

My Work

My photography journey started in 2011. For my birthday, I received a camera from my stepmother—a Lumix bridge camera. Nothing special, but perfect for my first steps. I started taking pictures during the holidays. We traveled to France, Norway, Poland, and Croatia. Later on, I began traveling by myself. Thousands and thousands of pictures, taken by me, each telling a story. But I wasn’t happy with the quality of my pictures. So, I started taking photography courses at KISP. I learned all the tips and tricks from the wonderful teachers Now,it’s time to show the world what I’ve developed over these years.

My Series

Intrigued by the photos above? Dive deeper below, where you’ll discover my narrative photography—series created during my education at KISP, each with its own story to tell. I’m passionate about weaving stories through series. They strengthen the impact of each image and spark the imagination. I hope you enjoy seeing these stories through my lens.

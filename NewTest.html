Humanity and It's Nature

My photography journey started in 2011. For my birthday, I received a camera from my stepmother—a Lumix bridge camera. Nothing special, but perfect for my first steps. I started taking pictures during the holidays. We traveled to France, Norway, Poland, and Croatia. Later on, I began traveling by myself. Thousands and thousands of pictures, taken by me, each telling a story. But I wasn’t happy with the quality of my pictures. So, I started taking photography courses at KISP. I learned all the tips and tricks from the wonderful teachers Now,it’s time to show the world what I’ve developed over these years.
